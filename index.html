STEP 1 — Copy your new email signature
1

Fill in your details and copy

Fill in your name, title, email and phone above → click Copy Signature button.

STEP 2 — Open Gmail signature settings
2

Go to Gmail settings

Open Gmail → click the ⚙ gear icon (top right) → click See all settings → click the General tab → scroll down to the Signature section.

STEP 3 — Create your new email signature
3

Create DSG New Email signature

Click Create new → name it DSG New Email → click Create → click inside the signature box on the right → select all existing text (Ctrl+A) and delete → press Ctrl+V to paste your copied signature.

If the logo does not appear immediately — click Save Changes first, then reopen settings. Gmail sometimes delays image rendering.

STEP 4 — Create your reply signature
4

Create DSG Reply signature

Click Create new again → name it DSG Reply → click Create → go back to the generator above → click Copy Reply Signature → click inside the DSG Reply box → press Ctrl+V to paste.

STEP 5 — Set signature defaults
5

Choose which signature appears where

Scroll to Signature defaults:
— For new emails use → select DSG New Email
— On reply/forward use → select DSG Reply

STEP 6 — Save
6

Save your settings

Scroll to the very bottom of the Settings page → click Save Changes.

Done! Send a test email to yourself to confirm your signature looks correct before emailing clients.
